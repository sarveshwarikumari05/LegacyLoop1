FAQ

How do I join the alumni network?

Click on the "Join Now" or "Register Now" button and fill out the registration form to become a member.

Is there any membership fee?

No, joining the alumni network is completely free for all graduates and students.

How can I contact other alumni?

Use the Alumni Directory feature to search and connect with fellow graduates.

Can I post job opportunities?

Yes, registered members can share job and internship opportunities on the Job Board.
